About me

I am a Computer Graphics and Deep Learning enthusiast.

I got my BSc in information engineering in 2019 and my MSc in ICT for internet and multimedia in 2021 at the University of Padova. Here I am actually a first-year Ph.D. student in information engineering and I work at the LTTM laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Simone Milani. My main research interests are in Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, Deep Learning and Autonomous Vehicles. Currently I am working on LiDAR Semantic Segmentation supervised by Dr. Umberto Michieli.

In 2015 I worked as a web developer at Technode S.R.L. and since September 2020 I am working as a Teaching Assistant for several master courses: 3D Augmented Reality (2 years), Digital Forensics (1 year), Digital and Interactive Multimedia (1 year), Usability and User Experience (1 year). In June 2021 I attended the Seasonal School in Artificial Intelligence, Extended Reality, and Robotics AIRONE at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna of Pisa, where I was awarded with a scholarschip grant offered by Fondazione il Talento all'Opera. In September 2021 my paper "Moving Drones for Wireless Coverage in a Three-Dimensional Grid Analyzed via Game Theory" has been published at APCCAS 2021 and selected among the best 7 presentations. In February 2022 my paper "Recent Advancements in Learning Algorithms for Point Clouds: An Updated Overview" has been published by MDPI Sensors.
